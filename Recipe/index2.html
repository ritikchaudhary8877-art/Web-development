Paneer Butter Masala

Paneer Butter Masala is a rich and creamy North Indian curry made with paneer, butter, and tomato gravy.

Ingredients

  • 200g Paneer
  • 2 Tomatoes (puree)
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 1 Onion (chopped)
  • Fresh Cream
  • Spices
